📋 4-Week Study Plan

Week 1 — Foundations / Fundamentos

  • Daily: 20 Quiz questions across all domains (short daily sessions > long sporadic ones).
  • Read Glossary terms for Domains A & B.
  • Review 10 Flashcards per day.

Week 2 — Core Domains / Dominios Clave

  • Focus Quiz filter on Domain C (Behavior Acquisition) and D (Behavior Reduction) — highest exam weight.
  • Complete 5 Clinical Cases per day.
  • Review missed questions from Week 1 (spaced repetition will prioritize them automatically).

Week 3 — Integration / Integración

  • Take your first full Simulator exam. Review every missed item's explanation.
  • Focus remaining time on your two weakest domains shown in Progress.

Week 4 — Mastery / Dominio

  • Daily Simulator exams. Goal: 80%+ on 3 consecutive simulators = ready for the real exam.
  • Light review only — avoid cramming new material the day before your exam.

5 Hardest Topics — Memory Tips

  • DRA vs DRI vs DRO vs DRL — DRA/DRI reinforce a replacement behavior; DRO reinforces absence of behavior; DRL reinforces lower rates.
  • Rate vs Frequency vs Percentage — rate = count ÷ time; always check the time unit requested.
  • Positive vs Negative Reinforcement/Punishment — positive = something added; negative = something removed; reinforcement increases, punishment decreases.
  • Confidentiality scenarios — the BEST answer almost never discloses client status, even indirectly.
  • IOA calculation methods — total count vs. interval-by-interval; know which formula applies to which data type.

Spaced Repetition Rules

  • Short daily sessions beat long sporadic ones.
  • Missed questions resurface more often until answered correctly multiple times.
  • Review the explanation every time, even on correct answers.
